About Us

ВКУС, КОТОРЫЙ ОБЪЕДИНЯЕТ

Pouring crimson tomatoes and fragrant onions, first-class beef and horse meat, the best spices, overflowing with the tastes of summer and sun - since 2015, the Aftondil team has brilliantly proved that in the hands of professionals, traditional oriental products can transform into pizza that wins the hearts of ardent gourmets. Translated from Uzbek, Aftondil means “messenger”. For seven years now, the Aftondil pizzeria chain has been confirming this status not only by the speed of delivering pizza with an oriental accent from the oven directly to your door, but also feels confident in the role of a messenger who brings good news and tastes at the junction of oriental values, European gastronomic traditions and cutting-edge technologies. The Aftondil Pizza brand, part of the Havoqand people group of companies, is successfully operating in the market of Uzbekistan and the CIS, systematically opening new branches. In 2021, we entered the Kazakhstan market, presenting our own vision of the pizzeria format to new consumers — cafes in which a traditional Italian dish turns into royal honors, prepared from the gifts of southern nature and generously seasoned with oriental hospitality.
